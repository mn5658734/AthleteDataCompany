Core Monetisation at Seed. Infrastructure at Scale.

We charge for access to intelligence that reduces commercial risk. At the Seed stage, we are ruthlessly focused on two revenue streams.

1 · Brand Intelligence SaaS

  • Who pays: Brand CMOs & sponsorship agencies
