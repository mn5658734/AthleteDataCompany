The Opportunity

The Athlete Economy Is Broken

🏃

Athletes

  • Less than 1% monetize before reaching elite level
  • No structured digital identity — visibility depends on selection, not merit
  • Uncertain career span with no financial upside from fan loyalty
🏢

Brands

  • No data-driven discovery at grassroots level
  • Poor ROI visibility in grassroots sponsorship spend
  • Campaign decisions based on gut feel, not audience alignment
👥

Fans

  • Passive consumption only — watch, scroll, forget
  • No ownership or long-term engagement with athlete growth
  • No stake in the athletes they already support

India has 600M+ sports fans and 1.5M+ grassroots athletes — but no connective tissue between them.

The Solution

ADC — Three Proprietary Engines, One Platform

Athlete Score Engine

Performance + consistency + growth potential → quantified into a single investable signal. Powers verified digital identity and brand discovery ranking.

Fan Signal Engine

Real-money behaviour on SportsTrade (trades, portfolio holds, repeat engagement) → converted into a Fan Conviction Score. Social media buzz layered on top.

Brand Match Engine

Data-driven athlete–brand pairing based on audience alignment, ROI potential, and campaign fit. Replaces manual scouting with AI-backed recommendations.
